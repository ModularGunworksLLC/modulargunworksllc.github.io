## What You Should Implement (Priority Order)

### PHASE 1: Primary Arms T&S Template (This Week)
Copy Primary Arms' T&S structure and adapt:

**File:** terms-and-conditions.html

**Required Sections:**
1. **Header**
   - State you're a firearms dealer
   - Clear age statement (18+ to visit, 21+ for handgun transactions)
   - Compliance disclaimer

2. **User Responsibility Section**
   - "You have familiarized yourself with laws of your state"
   - "It is YOUR responsibility to comply with federal, state, local laws"
   - "You agree to indemnify and hold harmless Modular Gunworks from all liability"

3. **Firearms-Specific Terms**
   - Age requirements (18+ rifles/shotguns, 21+ handguns)
   - FFL transfer requirements
   - Background check acknowledgment
   - Form 4473 explanation

4. **Liability Limitations**
   - "Products provided 'as is'"
   - "All information for general purposes only, not legal advice"
   - "Consult attorney for legal matters"

5. **Order Restrictions**
   - **For Alabama specifically:**
     - Handgun age: 21+
     - Rifle/Shotgun age: 18+
     - No Post Office box shipments for firearms
     - FFL verification required
   - **State-specific items:**
     - Any Alabama knife restrictions
     - Any Alabama ammunition restrictions

6. **Returns/Cancellations**
   - Take PSA's approach for firearms restocking
   - FFL transfer fees are customer responsibility

7. **SMS/Marketing Terms** (if applicable)
   - Opt-in/opt-out procedures
   - Message frequency
   - Carrier disclaimers

8. **Amendment II** (Add at bottom)
   - Quote the Second Amendment (like PSA does)
   - Shows patriotic commitment
