About Modular Gunworks LLC

Veteran-Owned Retail Excellence

We exist because the firearms retail industry needed less noise and more integrity. We cut through the clutter, deliver quality products, and treat every customer like they actually matter.

Built on Discipline

Michael Reams founded Modular Gunworks with the same mindset that defined his military service: build clean systems, operate with integrity, and never cut corners.

That philosophy shapes everything we do. We don't stock inventory just to move it. We source products we'd actually use ourselves. We communicate transparently about what works, what doesn't, and why it matters. We handle your orders with the same precision we'd want for our own.

This isn't a sales pitch. It's how we operate. You're not buying from a faceless vendor. You're buying from someone who understands the mission and refuses to compromise on standards.

What We Do Right Now

We focus on what we do best: curating and delivering quality ammunition, tactical gear, optics, and firearm components from trusted distributors and manufacturers.

Our selection is intentional, not bloated. Our pricing is competitive. Our support is real. When you order from us, you're getting products that have been vetted, from a retailer who actually cares about your satisfaction.

Our Operating Principles

Integrity

No shortcuts. No misrepresentation. You get honest information about products, pricing, and what to expect. That's non-negotiable.

Precision

Every product is selected with purpose. Every order is processed with care. We believe in doing things right the first time.

Reliability

You can depend on us. From product quality to shipping timelines to customer support—we show up and deliver.

Why Buy From Us

Veteran-Owned Perspective: We understand discipline, standards, and doing things right.
Curated Inventory: Quality over quantity. We stock what we'd recommend to friends.
Transparent Pricing: Fair prices with no hidden agendas.
Real Support: Questions answered by people who actually know the products.
Compliance First: We handle every transaction with legal and safety standards as non-negotiable.
Built for Growth: Future services (gunsmithing, FFL transfers) are coming as we scale.

Looking Ahead

Right now, we're perfecting retail. Down the road, as we grow, we'll expand into gunsmithing and professional services. But we won't rush it. Everything we add will meet the same standards that define us today.

Our goal isn't to be the biggest. It's to be the most reliable, most honest, most professional option for shooters and enthusiasts in Huntsville and beyond.
