About Modular Gunworks LLC

Our Mission

Modular Gunworks LLC is a veteran-owned Federal Firearms Licensee (FFL) based in Huntsville, Alabama. Our mission is to provide a clean, reliable, and professional storefront experience for firearms, suppressors, optics, ammunition, and tactical gear.

Our Story

Founded by veterans with decades of experience in the firearms industry, Modular Gunworks was built to serve the needs of responsible gun owners. We focus on quality products, exceptional customer service, and compliance with all federal and state regulations.

Our Values

  • Integrity: Honest business practices and transparent pricing.
  • Quality: Only the best products from trusted manufacturers.
  • Service: Personalized support for all your firearms needs.
  • Community: Supporting veterans and responsible gun ownership.
