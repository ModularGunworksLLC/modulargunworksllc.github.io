Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

Do you ship nationwide?

Yes, we ship to most states. Please note that firearms, ammunition, and certain items are subject to state and local restrictions. We comply with all applicable laws and regulations.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy on most items in original condition. Firearms and ammunition may have additional restrictions. Contact us for specific details.

How long does gunsmithing take?

Turnaround time depends on the complexity of the work. Simple repairs typically take 1-2 weeks, while custom builds may take 4-8 weeks. We'll provide an estimate when you drop off your firearm.

What is your FFL transfer process?

Contact us with the firearm details, complete required paperwork, undergo background check, and pick up after the waiting period. We handle all compliance and documentation.

Do you offer price matching?

We offer competitive pricing on all our products. Contact us if you find a better price on an identical item from an authorized retailer.

Are your products covered by warranty?

Most firearms come with manufacturer warranties. Accessories and gear have varying warranties depending on the brand. We're happy to explain warranty details for specific products.
