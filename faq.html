Shipping & Returns

Yes, we ship to most states. Please note that firearms, ammunition, and certain regulated items are subject to state and local restrictions. We comply with all applicable laws and will inform you of any restrictions for your area during checkout.

We offer a 30-day return policy on most items in original, unused condition. Firearms and ammunition may have additional restrictions or cannot be returned per federal law. Contact us for specific details on any item you wish to return.

Shipping costs depend on the weight, size, and destination of your order. We offer competitive shipping rates through major carriers. Costs will be calculated and displayed before you complete your purchase.

You'll receive a tracking number via email when your order ships. Use this number to track your package through the carrier's website. If you don't receive a tracking number, contact us immediately.

If your order arrives damaged, contact us immediately with photos of the damage and packaging. We'll work with you to either replace the item or issue a full refund. Please don't discard the packaging - we may need to file a claim with the carrier.
