FFL Transfers

Modular Gunworks LLC is a licensed Federal Firearms Licensee (FFL) in Huntsville, Alabama. We offer professional FFL transfer services for firearms purchased online or from out-of-state sellers.

Now available

Transfer fees

$20 — first firearm

$10 — each additional firearm (when multiple guns are transferred at the same time)

The $10 rate applies only when you are having more than one firearm transferred in the same transaction (e.g., multiple guns from the same shipment or picked up together). Single transfers are $20. Standard transfers only (rifles, shotguns, handguns); we do not currently process NFA items.

How it works

  • Have your firearm shipped to Modular Gunworks LLC (use our address as the FFL ship-to when ordering).
  • We will contact you when your firearm arrives.
  • Come to our Huntsville location with a valid ID to complete the transfer and required background check (Form 4473 / NICS).
  • Pay the transfer fee at pickup (see pricing above).

What you need

Valid government-issued photo ID. You must be legally eligible to possess firearms under federal and state law. Alabama residents: we can complete the transfer at our location. Out-of-state buyers: firearms must ship to an FFL in your state of residence—we cannot transfer to you in Alabama if you are not an Alabama resident.
