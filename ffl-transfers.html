Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a transfer typically take?

Most transfers complete in 1-3 business days after the firearm arrives. The NICS background check usually takes 15 minutes to a few hours. We can often complete same-day transfers if the item arrives and you visit on the same day.

Do you accept transfers from all online retailers?

Yes! We accept transfers from any licensed dealer, marketplace, or even private sellers. As long as the item is legal in Alabama and the seller can legally ship it, we can handle the transfer. Just provide us with the seller's info.

What if my background check doesn't clear?

If your background check is delayed or denied, you have the option to appeal or request a manual review. We're happy to help with this process. The firearm remains securely stored with us while we await resolution.

Can I transfer items from out of state?

Some items can be shipped out of state; others cannot. Handguns must generally be shipped to the purchaser's home state's FFL. We can advise you on specific items. For rifles and shotguns, out-of-state transfers are usually no problem.

Do you store firearms while waiting for pickup?

Absolutely. We securely store all incoming firearms in our vault at no extra charge. They're insured and protected. You can pick up whenever it's convenient for you, within 30 days of arrival.

Can someone else pick up the firearm for me?

No, federal law requires that the transferee (the person whose name is on the background check) be present for pickup. However, spouses living at the same residence may be able to take possession of certain firearms. Call us to discuss your specific situation.
