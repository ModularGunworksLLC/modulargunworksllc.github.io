Return Policy

We want you to be satisfied with your purchase. Non-defective items in resalable condition may be returned within 30 days of receipt for replacement or refund (less original shipping).

  • 30-day return window from date of delivery
  • Items must be unused, in original condition, with all packaging
  • Refund or exchange (subject to availability) after we receive and inspect the item
  • Items returned with use or damage may be subject to a 15% restocking fee

Final Sale – No Returns

The following items are final sale and cannot be returned, exchanged, or refunded:

  • Ammunition (all types) – federal regulations and safety
  • Firearms, frames, and receivers – once transferred to an FFL
  • Ammunition components and reloading supplies (primers, powder, loaded ammo)
  • Used, worn, or fired items
  • Clearance or “blem” items (unless defective)
  • Customized or custom-ordered items

How to Initiate a Return

For eligible items only:

  1. Contact us at sales@modulargunworks.com or (256) 384-3852 with your order number and reason for return.
  2. We will provide return instructions and, if applicable, any return authorization.
  3. Ship the item back in original condition. Return shipping is the customer’s responsibility unless the return is due to our error or a defective product.
  4. Refunds are processed within 7 business days after we receive and approve the return.

Defective Products

If you receive a defective item (manufacturing defect, not user damage), contact us within 30 days with your order number and, if requested, photos or description. We will replace or refund as appropriate. See our Terms of Service for full details.
