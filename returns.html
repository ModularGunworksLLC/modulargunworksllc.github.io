Returns & Exchanges

Return Policy

At Modular Gunworks LLC, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with an item, we offer hassle-free returns within 30 days of purchase.

  • 30-day return window from date of purchase
  • Items must be in original condition with all packaging
  • Full refund or exchange available
  • Firearms and ammunition subject to additional restrictions

How to Initiate a Return

Returning an item is simple:

  1. Contact our customer service team at support@modulargunworks.com
  2. Provide your order number and reason for return
  3. Receive return shipping label and instructions
  4. Ship item back to us in original condition
  5. Receive refund within 7 business days of receiving return

Restricted Items

Please note that certain items have special return policies due to legal restrictions:

  • Firearms: Must comply with all federal and state regulations
  • Ammunition: Non-returnable due to safety regulations
  • Customized Items: Non-returnable if custom ordered
