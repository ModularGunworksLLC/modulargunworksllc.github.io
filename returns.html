Returns & Exchanges

Quick Summary

We accept non-firearm product returns for up to 7 days from the date of delivery!

As long as the items are in a saleable condition (if they look like they could be on a store shelf you have nothing to worry about) we will issue a refund or credit (your choice), less any applicable restocking fees.

Except in the case of damaged-in-transit items, all refunds are subject to a restocking fee of 20% of the original purchase price, less the original shipping costs.

All sealed items, like spam cans, cannot be returned once opened unless the items are defective.

What about manufacturing defects?

While the manufacturers want you to deal directly with them, we don't! Talking to manufacturers is frustrating, and I promise we are better at it than you are.

If you have a problem with any items you have purchased from us, please tell us and let us deal with it. You have better things to do!

*All Food Items Are Non-returnable.

Returns & Exchanges for Firearms

If a firearm that ships to your selected FFL is returned by the FFL to us for any reason (i.e. Failed background check or firearm is not allowed in state or municipality) you will be subject to a 20% restocking fee.

Once a firearm is transferred from the FFL to the buyer, there are no returns allowed.

Modifications & Cancellations

We are unable to modify or cancel orders once they are processing through our warehouse.

If your order has already been packed, we cannot cancel the order. This policy helps ensure all orders will go out quickly.

Please see our return policy if you would like to send your order back.

General Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you are not satisfied, you may return most new, unopened items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund, subject to the conditions below.

  • All returns must be authorized. Please contact us at modulargunworks@gmail.com to initiate a return.
  • Items must be returned in their original packaging and condition.
  • Firearms, ammunition, and certain special order items are not eligible for return unless defective or damaged in shipping.
  • Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the return is due to our error.
  • Refunds are processed within 5 business days after we receive and inspect your return.

Exchanges

If you need to exchange an item for the same product, contact us for instructions. Exchanges are subject to product availability.

Non-Returnable Items

  • Opened ammunition
  • Firearms (unless defective, see above)
  • Special order or custom items
  • Gift cards

Questions?

Contact our support team at modulargunworks@gmail.com for any questions about returns or exchanges.
